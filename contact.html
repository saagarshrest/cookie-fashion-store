FREQUENTLY ASKED

HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to monitor your delivery status in real-time.

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

DO YOU OFFER INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All duties and taxes are included in the final price at checkout.

HOW DO I CARE FOR MY COOKIE GARMENTS?

Each garment comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning or gentle machine washing in cold water to preserve quality.

CAN I BOOK A PRIVATE STYLING APPOINTMENT?

Absolutely! Contact our stores directly to schedule a one-on-one styling session with our expert team.
